1. Introduction / Who We Are

Aetryx ("Aetryx", "we", "us", or "our") develops and operates mobile applications and digital services. This policy applies to all current and future Aetryx apps unless an app provides a separate policy that supersedes this one for specific data practices.

By using our apps or services, you acknowledge the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.

If a specific app has additional privacy details, those details supplement this policy.

2. What Data Aetryx Apps May Collect

Depending on app features, Aetryx apps may collect or process the following categories of data:

  • Account & Identity Data: name, email address, user ID, authentication tokens.
  • User Content: notes, entries, uploads, settings, and in-app content you create.
  • Device & Technical Data: device type, operating system, app version, language, crash logs, diagnostics, and performance telemetry.
  • Usage Data: feature interactions, session activity, and engagement signals.
  • Purchase Data: subscription status, transaction receipts, and entitlement information (processed by Apple/Google or payment processors).
  • Support Data: messages, screenshots, and details you provide to customer support.
  • Optional Sensitive Data (Feature-Dependent): information you choose to store for app functionality (for example, personal records, reminders, or documents).

Some apps may operate primarily on-device and collect minimal or no personal data beyond what is required for core functionality.

Aetryx does not knowingly collect data beyond what is necessary for app operation, improvement, security, legal compliance, and support.

3. How Data Is Used

We use data to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve app functionality.
  • Personalize user experience and settings.
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts.
  • Process subscriptions and purchases.
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, abuse, and technical issues.
  • Respond to support requests and communicate service updates.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

Where required by law, we process personal data under appropriate legal bases (such as consent, contract performance, legal obligations, or legitimate interests).

4. Third-Party Services (Analytics, Ads, APIs)

Aetryx apps may use third-party providers to operate services, including:

  • Analytics Providers for aggregated usage metrics and diagnostics.
  • Advertising Networks (only in apps that include ads).
  • Cloud/Infrastructure Providers for hosting, storage, and delivery.
  • Authentication or API Providers for account, identity, and external integrations.
  • Payment Platforms (such as Apple App Store / Google Play billing).

Third-party providers may process data under their own privacy policies and contractual obligations with Aetryx.

We do not sell personal information for money. If data sharing for advertising or analytics qualifies as "sharing" under local law, users may have rights to opt out (see Section 7).

5. Data Retention Policy

We retain data only for as long as necessary to fulfill legitimate business or legal purposes, including:

  • Maintaining active user accounts and service continuity.
  • Providing customer support and troubleshooting.
  • Meeting tax, accounting, legal, and compliance obligations.

Retention periods vary by data category and app. When data is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it where reasonably possible.

In apps that store data locally on your device, you may remove data by uninstalling the app and/or using in-app delete options, unless backup copies persist in your own cloud backups.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Aetryx apps are not directed to children under 13 unless explicitly stated in the app listing. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent where required by law.

If you believe a child has provided personal information in violation of applicable law, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to investigate and delete such information.

7. User Rights (GDPR / CCPA and Similar Laws)

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Right to know/access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to request deletion of personal data.
  • Right to data portability (where applicable).
  • Right to object or restrict certain processing (where applicable).
  • Right to opt out of certain data sharing/targeted advertising (where applicable).
  • Right not to receive discriminatory treatment for exercising privacy rights.

To exercise rights, contact us using the details in Section 9. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling requests.

Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their devices and account credentials.
